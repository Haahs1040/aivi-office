How it works

Three agents. One quiet overnight run.

AIVI OFFICE runs on a team of AI agents, each specialized for a piece of the bookkeeping workflow your staff already knows. They hand off to each other — and to your humans — with a clear audit trail.

01

Data entry agent

Pulls transactions from connected bank feeds, ingests receipts and invoices, and gets every line into QuickBooks in the right shape.

  • Bank & card feeds
  • Receipt + invoice OCR
  • Duplicate detection
02

Categorization agent

Applies your firm's chart-of-accounts conventions per client. Learns from your bookkeepers' corrections — not from a generic model.

  • Per-client rules
  • Vendor memory
  • Confidence-scored flags
03

Reconciliation agent

Matches transactions to statements, surfaces mismatches, and drafts the month-end close for a human to approve — not redo.

  • Statement matching
  • Variance explanations
  • Close-ready workpapers
